Intresting Facts about Chameleons

  1. Chameleons are reptiles that are part of iguana suborder
  2. Changing skin colour is an important part of communnication among chameleons
  3. Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Intresting Facts about Pigeons

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  2. Pigeons are renowed for their outstanding navigational abilities
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Intresting Facts about Camels

  1. There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  2. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  3. Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.

Intresting Facts about Cicada

  1. Females cicadas may be attracted to the sound of motors
  2. The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus
  3. Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs.

Intresting Facts about Panda

  1. Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown)
  2. An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!
  3. Pandas are "lazy" — eating and sleeping make their day
